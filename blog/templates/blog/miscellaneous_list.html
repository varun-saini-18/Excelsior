About Excelsior

Power of the Pen is an interscholastic writing league founded by Lorraine B. Merrill in 1986. It is a non-profit creative writing program for students in grades seven and eight in the U.S. state of Ohio.

An online celebration of the 2020 statewide awards is still available for viewing! We announce the winners of the 2020 Poetry Contest and Powerful Pen, Promising Young Talent, and Humor awards. Regional Best of the Best is announced, as well. Guest author Margaret Peterson Haddix, poet George Bilgere, and television writer Sage Boggs all appear on video to honor Power of the Pen writers!
